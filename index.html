Mission Statement

Gym of Knowledge is a personal training facility that dedicates itself to providing customers with state of the art fitness instructions, training methods, on exercise equipment. We strive to give our clients an accepting and educational environment that they can work out in.

Vision Statement

To become a gym that makes clients better both intellectually and physically.

Service

Educate clients by providing them with proper physical training, and the purposes of each type of gym equipment. Our services can be viewed as courses in which clients learn plenty of very useful information regarding how to become fit. Personal trainers will instruct clients, no matter how knowledgeable they may or not already be, at the level of physical education they prefer. Trainers will demonstrate in front of clients how the various workouts should be done, in order that they may have a proper guide to follow. Following them, clients will attempt to perform the same exercise at a comfortable pace. Trainers will critique their form objectively, with no added pressure, only words of encouragement.
