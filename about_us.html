About Us

We at Gym of Knowledge are people pursuing the betterment of the body as well as the betterment of the mind. We are a gym that accepts anyone no matter where you're at in the development of oneself. There are staff on hand willing to help at a moment's notice and you will always feel comfortable bettering yourself at your own pace.

Our main headquarters are located at 698 Triton Avenue, New York, 12347. The headquarters overlook Hong Kong's iconic harbor and New York's skyline coast. This provides our clients with an overall stunning panoramic view of the Tsim Sha Tsui and Empire State building. Furthermore, our customers may visit any one of our other facilities: located in places such as, Dubai, Switzerland, France, and Russia.
